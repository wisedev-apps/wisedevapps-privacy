Delete your Timely account

Timely (com.wisedevapps.alarm) lets you delete your account and all associated data at any time, either directly in the app or by emailing us.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (immediate)

  1. Open Timely and go to Settings.
  2. Scroll to Privacy and tap Delete account.
  3. Type DELETE to confirm. You may be asked to sign in again if your last sign-in was a while ago.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email wisedev.apps@gmail.com from the address linked to your Timely account with the subject "Timely account deletion request". We verify ownership of the account and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile, email address, display name, and profile photo
  • All wake-up requests, voice/video messages, and thank-you notes
  • Friend connections and friend requests
  • Your settings, alarms synced to your account, and analytics identifiers
  • Your subscription record with our billing processor (RevenueCat); manage or cancel the subscription itself in Google Play subscriptions

What is retained, and for how long

  • Abuse reports where your account was reported by another user are retained as moderation evidence after deletion. They contain the report metadata only, not your messages.
  • Standard server backup copies are purged on the backup provider's rotation schedule (at most 30 days).

Appeals

If your account was disabled following an abuse report and you believe this was a mistake, email wisedev.apps@gmail.com with the subject "Timely moderation appeal". A human reviews every appeal and replies within 14 days.
