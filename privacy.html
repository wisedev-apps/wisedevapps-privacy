Privacy Policy

App: Timely (com.wisedevapps.alarm)
Developer: WiseDev Apps (wisedev.apps@gmail.com)
Effective date: 11 June 2026

This policy explains what data Timely collects, why, and what rights you have over it. Timely is an alarm clock with an optional social wake-up feature that lets a trusted friend record a voice or video message that plays when your alarm fires.

1. Who this policy applies to

Timely is intended for users aged 13 and over. We ask for your date of birth before account creation. If you are under 13 you can still use Timely as a local alarm clock (guest mode) — no account is created and no personal data is collected server-side.

2. Data we collect and why

2a. Account data

When you create an account via Google Sign-In or email:

  • Email address and display name — to identify your account and let friends find you by invite link or QR code.
  • Profile photo (optional) — shown to your friends.
  • Date of birth — verified at sign-up to enforce the 13+ age requirement. Only the result (eligible / not eligible) is stored; the raw date is not retained.

This data is stored in Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore (Google LLC). See Firebase Privacy and Security.

2b. Social wake-up feature data

If you use the friend wake-up feature:

  • Wake-up requests — metadata (which alarm, which friend, scheduled time) stored in Cloud Firestore.
  • Voice and video messages — recorded by your helper friend, stored in Firebase Storage (Google LLC). Messages are end-to-end encrypted: encrypted on your friend's device using your public key (X25519 ECDH + AES-256-GCM) before upload. The decryption key never leaves your device and is held exclusively in Android's hardware-backed secure storage. We cannot read your messages. If you reinstall the app, your decryption key is permanently lost and in-flight messages cannot be recovered — this is by design.
  • Thank-you notes — short text messages stored in Cloud Firestore.

2c. Friend connections

Friend requests and accepted friend connections are stored in Cloud Firestore. Friends can only be added by sharing an invite link or scanning a QR code — there is no searchable directory. Friend connections are mutual and require acceptance by both parties.

2d. Alarms

Your alarm schedules are stored locally on your device. If you are signed in, alarm metadata (time, repeat rule, label) may also be stored in Cloud Firestore to support cross-device restoration — but the alarm sound and local mission configuration stay on-device only.

2e. Analytics and crash reporting

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) to understand how the app is used and to fix crashes.

  • In regions where GDPR or CCPA applies, analytics and crash reporting are off by default until you consent via the data-consent prompt shown on first launch.
  • You can withdraw consent at any time in Settings → Privacy → Share usage data.
  • Outside GDPR/CCPA regions, analytics are on by default; the same opt-out is available.

2f. Subscriptions

Purchases and subscription status are managed through Google Play Billing and RevenueCat (RevenueCat Inc.). RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous user ID and your purchase history to validate entitlements. We do not receive or store your payment card details. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

2g. Advertising

The free tier shows ads served by Appodeal (BidMachine Inc.) and its demand partners. Appodeal may collect device identifiers (including the Android Advertising ID) to serve personalised ads.

  • In regions where GDPR or CCPA applies, a consent form is shown before any ad data is collected. You can decline personalised ads and still use the app.
  • Ads never appear during the alarm ring sequence or mission flow.
  • Premium subscribers see no ads.

For the full list of Appodeal's demand partners, see the Appodeal Privacy Policy.

2h. Push notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) (Google LLC) to deliver social wake-up notifications (friend accepted your request, message recorded, etc.). FCM requires a device token; this token is stored in Cloud Firestore against your account and is deleted when your account is deleted.

2i. Step counting

One optional alarm-dismissal mission counts your steps using the device's pedometer. Step data is processed entirely on-device and is never stored or transmitted.

2j. Clipboard

When you open the Add Friend screen, Timely reads your clipboard once to detect whether you have copied a Timely invite link. On Android 12 and above, the system shows a notification when this happens. No clipboard content is stored or transmitted.

3. Data we do not collect

Timely does not access your contacts, call logs, SMS messages, or location. Camera and microphone access is used only when you are actively recording a wake-up message.

5. Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account at any time:

  • In the app: Settings → Privacy → Delete account → type DELETE to confirm.
  • By email: send a request to wisedev.apps@gmail.com from the address linked to your account. We complete the deletion within 30 days.

Deletion removes your profile, messages, friend connections, subscription record, and analytics identifiers. Full instructions and details of what is (and is not) deleted are on the Delete your account page.

Data export

You can request an export of your data under GDPR Article 20. Go to Settings → Privacy → Export my data. You will receive a JSON file containing your profile, alarm schedules, and friend list. Encrypted voice/video messages are excluded (we cannot decrypt them). Export is rate-limited to once per 24 hours.

7. Children's privacy

Timely is not directed at children under 13. Users under 13 are restricted to guest mode (local alarms only) with no account creation and no server-side data collection. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us at wisedev.apps@gmail.com and we will delete it.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email wisedev.apps@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

9. Security

All data is transmitted over TLS. Voice and video messages are end-to-end encrypted (see §2b). Firebase Security Rules restrict every collection to authenticated owners and authorised friends only. We do not use cleartext network traffic.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will change. For material changes, we will notify you in the app.
